Paragon Autonomous is a venture dedicated to mitigating the increase in frequency and destruction of wildfires. Our solution is twofold: an autonomous scouting drone built to detect fires in real-time and a payload drone to drop fire retardant and assist first responders.

Our Services

Proactive Monitoring

In Arizona, detection of deadly wildfire often rely on people call-ins. Paragon Autonomous’s drones will actively scout for wildfires using advanced thermal imaging and computer vision technology.

Firefighting Aid

Paragon Autonomous’s payload drone will be equipped with up to 10 pounds of fire retardant to assist with small fires before emergency assistance arrives.

Aerial Assistance and Imaging

Our scouting drone acts as eyes in the sky with thermal imaging and camera technology, allowing first responders to assess the scene in real-time before they arrive.
